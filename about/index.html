About Preston

Web designer, SEO strategist, and small business advocate.

Hey, I'm Preston.

I started MetaVerse Marketer because I was tired of seeing small businesses get taken advantage of by overpriced agencies and underperforming websites.

With a background in digital marketing and a degree in the field, I bring a unique combination of technical skill and strategic thinking to every project. I don't just build websites — I build marketing machines that attract, engage, and convert.

I've worked with businesses across industries: dental practices, construction companies, e-commerce stores, authors, coaches, and more. The common thread? They all needed a web presence that actually worked for their business goals.

My Approach

I believe in honest marketing. No gimmicks, no "growth hacks" that don't last. Just solid strategy, clean design, and SEO that compounds over time.

Every project starts with a conversation — I want to understand your business, your customers, and your goals before I write a single line of code.

Based in Utah

I'm located in the Silicon Slopes area and work with clients nationwide. Whether you're a local business in Utah or a company across the country, I'd love to help you grow.
