About

I'm Preston Vawdrey.

Web designer, SEO consultant, marketing strategist. I help businesses build websites that actually work.

Preston Vawdrey

Marketing-first. Always.

I came at web design from the marketing side. Started in digital marketing, learned that most businesses had websites that looked okay but did nothing for them, and built my practice around fixing that gap.

The name "MetaVerse Marketer" came from a simple idea: marketing in the digital age has gotten so complicated that every channel feels like its own universe. SEO, content, paid ads, email, social media. You don't need to master all of them. You need someone who can help you pick the right ones and execute well.

I work with businesses directly. No project managers, no outsourced teams. When you hire me, you get me. WordPress, Shopify, SEO, copywriting. I do the work myself.

Based in Utah. Working with clients across the US.

My approach.

Listen first

Every project starts with understanding your business, your customers, and your goals. Not a template. Not assumptions. Actual conversations about what you need.

Build with intent

Every design decision serves a purpose. The layout, the copy, the calls to action. All designed to move visitors toward becoming customers.

Communicate clearly

You'll never wonder what's happening with your project. Regular updates, draft reviews, and straight talk about what's working and what isn't.
