Hey, I'm Preston.

I'm a digital marketing professional who helps businesses, entrepreneurs, and content creators build websites that make sense for their business.

I call myself the "Metaverse Marketer" because I think of marketing as a stackable universe of options you can use to grow your business. Some businesses grow through SEO, others through community building, content creation, paid ads, or traditional advertising. The secret is that almost all of these avenues are viable — but you need to be careful how you implement them.

That's what makes the marketing world feel like a Metaverse — and that's where I come in.

Why "Metaverse Marketer"?

Marketing in the digital age has gotten complicated. Every marketing channel feels like a universe you need to master. Depending on your business, you may need to master a multitude of them — kinda like a metaverse.

My philosophy is to help you master the marketing channels that lead to actual business success. Not vanity metrics, not trendy tactics — real, measurable results.

Marketing used to be "Mad Men" style mass communication. But then everyone got phones. Now everyone is a customer to everyone else. Whether you're buying political opinions or selling designer clothes, everyone constantly participates in our new "meta."
