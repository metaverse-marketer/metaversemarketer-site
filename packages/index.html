Common questions.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes. You can upgrade or change your plan at any time. We'll adjust the scope and pricing accordingly.

What if I need something not listed?

The packages cover the most common needs. If you need something specific (CRM integration, email marketing setup, custom development), let's talk during the strategy call and I'll build a custom quote.

Do you offer refunds?

For one-time builds, I work in milestones and you approve each one before we proceed. If we're not a good fit after the first milestone, we can part ways. Monthly retainers can be cancelled with 30 days notice.

What's the payment process?

One-time builds: 50% upfront, 50% on delivery. Monthly retainers: billed at the start of each month. I'll send invoices and you can pay via card or bank transfer.

Not sure which plan fits?

That's what the strategy call is for. We'll figure out the right scope together.
