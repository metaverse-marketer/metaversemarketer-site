Pricing FAQ

What's the difference between one-time and monthly?

One-time packages are for building or redesigning your website. Monthly packages are for ongoing support, updates, SEO, and strategic guidance. Many clients start with a one-time build and then move to a monthly plan.

Can I switch plans later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel monthly plans at any time. No long-term contracts.

Do you offer refunds?

If you're not satisfied with the work, I'll make it right. I offer revision rounds on all packages so you're happy with the final result before launch.
