Questions about SEO.

How long until I see ROI from SEO?

For relatively easy keywords, I've gotten clients ranking in the top 5 within 3-6 months. ROI depends on your product and the competitiveness of your market. We'll discuss realistic expectations on the strategy call.

Do you guarantee rankings?

No legitimate SEO consultant guarantees specific rankings because Google's algorithm isn't something anyone controls. What I guarantee is a data-driven strategy, transparent reporting, and consistent effort. The results follow.

Is SEO better than paid ads?

They serve different purposes. Paid ads are instant but stop when you stop paying. SEO takes time but compounds over months and years. Most businesses benefit from both. I can help you figure out the right mix.

What's included in monthly SEO?

Keyword tracking, content recommendations, on-page tweaks, technical monitoring, competitor analysis, and a monthly report. It depends on your package. We'll scope it during the call.
