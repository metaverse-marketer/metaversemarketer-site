My SEO Process

SEO Audit

I analyze your current site — technical health, content gaps, keyword opportunities, and what your competitors are doing. You get a clear report of what's working and what's not.

Keyword Research

Not just "high volume" keywords — I find the terms your actual customers are searching for. The ones that signal buying intent and match your services.

On-Page Optimization

Title tags, meta descriptions, heading structure, internal linking, schema markup — the fundamentals done right. This alone can move the needle significantly.

Content Strategy

Blog posts, service pages, and landing pages built around your target keywords. Content that answers real questions and establishes your authority.

Ongoing Optimization

SEO isn't a one-and-done thing. I track rankings, analyze what's working, and continuously refine your strategy. Monthly reports so you always know where you stand.
