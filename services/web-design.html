WordPress vs. Shopify: which one?

I work with both platforms. Here's how I decide which one to recommend.

WordPress

Best for service businesses

Law firms, consultants, construction companies, healthcare practices, personal brands. If you sell services, WordPress gives you the flexibility and SEO power you need.

Also works for WooCommerce eCommerce when you need WordPress-level customization for your store.

Shopify

Best for product businesses

Physical products, subscription boxes, retail brands. If you sell things, Shopify is the most reliable platform with the best integration for ads, social media, and fulfillment.

Easier to manage day-to-day than WordPress/WooCommerce for most business owners.
