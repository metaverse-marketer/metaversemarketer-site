Frequently Asked Questions

How much do freelance web designers charge?

For my clients, I charge by the page and offer a 5-page package starting at $499. Pricing varies based on your needs, hosting, theme, and existing content. I'm always transparent about costs upfront.

Do you help with website hosting?

Yes. I can either help you manage your own hosting or provide managed hosting on my partner service for a monthly fee. Either way, I make sure your site loads fast and stays secure.

Is WordPress or Shopify better for eCommerce?

For eCommerce, I recommend Shopify. It's more user-friendly, easier to integrate with marketing channels like TikTok, YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, and Google, and gets your business up and running faster.

How long until I see SEO results?

It depends on your industry and target keywords. For relatively easy keywords, I've gotten clients ranking in the top 5 search results in about 3–6 months. ROI depends on your product or service.

Why is web design important?

Your website is the central marketing hub for all your promotional activities. If it's poorly designed or not optimized for conversions, you risk wasting money on traffic that doesn't convert into customers.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Absolutely. I offer monthly maintenance packages that include web design assistance, strategy calls, analytics review, and more. Many of my clients have been with me for over a year.
